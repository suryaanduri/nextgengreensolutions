About Us

Nextgen Green Solutions is a leading provider of engineering design and validation services, specializing in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. With a strong focus on delivering world-class Pharma Facilities, our team of skilled engineers is dedicated to taking projects from concept to operation, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and providing cost-effective designs.

Our mission is to deliver expert engineering solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency. We are committed to supporting the pharmaceutical manufacturing process through comprehensive validation services, ensuring that all systems and equipment meet GxP (Good Practice) and non-GxP standards.

  • Automation Support
  • PLC and SCADA Validation as per GAMP Requirements
  • Application Software Validation as per GAMP and EU Annex 11
  • EMS/BMS Qualification
